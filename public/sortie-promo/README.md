# Sortie Promo - Media

Place your sortie promo images and videos in this directory.

## Required Media:
Add photos and videos from the promotional outing/cohesion event.

## Recommended Format:
- **Images**: JPG or PNG
- **Videos**: MP4 or MOV
- Optimized for web (compressed but good quality)

## Naming Convention (suggested):
- `sortie_promo_001.jpg`
- `sortie_promo_002.jpg`
- `sortie_promo_video_01.mp4`
- etc.

## Note:
These media will be displayed in a Swiper carousel in the SortiePromo section of the GI 2025 Wrapped experience, similar to the PromoShoot section.

## Example Files to Add:
- Group photos
- Candid moments
- Activity photos
- Fun moments
- Team bonding
- Videos of the outing
